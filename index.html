Steps to write a song

  • Recommended DAW
  • Sell Your Music

Step one

I like to think about the genre and who am I singing this song to, who is my audience?

Step two

I start by composing a simple melody on guitar, usually the chorus of the song just to get an idea about the key, BPM (Beats Per Minutes)

Step three

I move to my DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) and start building the beat, composing the melodies, recording instruments and adding electronic instruments while writing the lyrics along the way.

Step four

Now I have the beat ready and the lyrics and it's time to record the vocals which is kind of the easiest part.

Finally

After that it's time for the final mixing and mastering of the song and BOOM! WE HAVE MADE A SONG!
